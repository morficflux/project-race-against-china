# Spec 000: Vision

**Status:** draft
**Authors:** Milton (game designer), Dad (scribe)

## What we're making

A side-scrolling car racing game that runs in a web browser. You drive a
hand-drawn car from left to right across bumpy, ramp-filled levels, crashing
through destructible obstacles, trying to reach the finish line.

## The three pillars (if a feature doesn't serve one, we cut it)

1. **Milton's art is the game.** Every car, obstacle, and background starts as
   a drawing on paper. Seeing his drawing drive around IS the product.
2. **Physics is the fun.** The car has real suspension, catches air off ramps,
   flips if you land badly. Obstacles tumble and bounce when hit.
3. **Smashing things feels great.** Crates, cones, walls break into pieces
   that fly with physics. Destruction is rewarded, not punished.

## What we are NOT making (yet)

- Multiplayer
- Mobile/touch (keyboard first)
- Saving/accounts
- More than one car until the first level is fun

## Definition of "version 1 done"

Milton can hand a laptop to a friend, they press arrow keys, drive a
Milton-drawn car through one full level, smash at least 3 kinds of
destructible things, and reach a finish line with a celebration screen.
